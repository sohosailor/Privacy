Privacy Policy for BandCapture

BandCapture does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Audio recordings created within the app are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to any external servers.

BandCapture does not require user accounts and does not track user activity.

If you choose to export audio files, they are transferred only to the destinations you select.

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising frameworks, or tracking technologies.
